Me and

the person opposite me

Hany (Me)

My name is Hany Koh, but my friends call me "Haniko." I'm 19 years old and I study Systems Engineering.

I've always liked computers, which is why I studied programming. I like gothic music and playing the violin. Although my career can be stressful, I enjoy it, and having good friends and a supportive partner makes everything easier. I love cats, the color blue, anime, and Hatsune Miku. I like to write in my free time, I write about myself and I also write poetry.

I really like video games, in fact I met Azula through one. Although I like video games, I'm not very good at them. I generally like calm games, like those that include challenges or puzzles, as well as indie games, but I'm very bad at games that require skill and dexterity. Azula always helps me improve or level up in those games.

My best friend Azula (The opposite of me)

Azula is 24 years old and has already finished her degree. She likes science, sweets, classical music, and playing the piano.

Unlike me, she doesn't really like programming, although she understands it. She prefers the color gold to blue and doesn't like Hatsune Miku, she also likes corn on the cob. While we both like anime, our tastes and interests differ in many ways. Azula loves making donuts.

Azula also likes video games, we met through one and have been playing together ever since. She's really good at games that involve skill. Unlike me, she likes competitive games where you have to be the best. She always manages to be the best.

Even with these differences, we get along, Azula is my best friend and we complement each other in unexpected ways!
