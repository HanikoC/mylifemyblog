About me

My name is Hany Koh, but my friends usually call me “Haniko”. I am 19 years old and currently studying Systems Engineering. I have loved computers since I was very young, which led me to study programming even before I started my degree.

I really like my career, although it can be quite stressful at times. The complexity of the subjects and the workload can be overwhelming, but having good friends by my side makes everything more manageable. They help lighten the load and make the journey more enjoyable. Plus, I have a wonderful partner who supports me in everything I do, which gives me extra motivation to keep going.

I live with my family and we share our home with two cats. I have always loved cats; they have a special place in me. Spending time with them helps me relax.

Despite the difficult degree, I am excited about the future and want to learn and grow more in this field.
